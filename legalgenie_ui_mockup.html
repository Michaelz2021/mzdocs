Philippine Labor Law Requirements

For restaurant employees, you must comply with DOLE minimum wage requirements. The current minimum wage in Metro Manila is ₱610 per day for non-agricultural workers.

Probationary Period

Under the Labor Code, the probationary period cannot exceed 6 months. During this time, you can terminate employment with just cause or when the employee fails to qualify as a regular employee.

Mandatory Benefits

All employees must be enrolled in SSS, PhilHealth, and Pag-IBIG. You're also required to provide 13th month pay and service incentive leave.
